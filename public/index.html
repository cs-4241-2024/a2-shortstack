Lap 1 Lap 2 Lap 3 Total Options

To start, use the arrow keys or WASD. Race the car around the track three times and a score will be submitted automatically.

There is a checkpoint at the bottom of the track that must be reached every lap. Try to avoid the grass, it slows you down!

Press the reset button at any time to start over, or start a new game after you complete a race.

Use the Delete button to delete a score from the table (and server). Use the shuffle button to shuffle lap times (for fun!).
